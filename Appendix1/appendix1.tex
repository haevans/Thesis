\chapter{Distributions of input variables for the global BDT}
\label{sec:appendix1}
Comparison of the signal and background distributions of the input variables used in the global BDT for 2011, 2012, 2015 and 2016 data taking conditions. Signal distributions are from simulated \bsmumu decays for each year that have passed the selection cuts in Table~\ref{tab:BDTpresel}. The background distributions are from \bbbarmumux decays in 2011, 2012, 2015 and 2016 data with $m_{\mu \mu} > 5447$ \m\
evcc and 2012 simulated \bbbarmumux decays passing the selection cuts in Table~\ref{tab:BDTpresel}.
